    Unflavoured

    How many serves a day?
    How much collagen you should take each day depends on your own health goals. Between 15-30grams of collagen peptides a day will give you visible results.

    One serve a day is ideal if you want to combat fine lines and wrinkles, improve your hair and nail strength, ease joint pain, build muscle, improve gut health and get an overall energy boost.

    Two serves a day will fast track your results. Have one serve morning and evening.

    Storage:Store in a cool, dark place out of direct sunlight.

    Is collagen safe for pregnant and nursing mothers?

    Yes, our collagen is perfectly safe for both pregnant and nursing mothers. In fact, there are many benefits of taking collagen whilst pregnant. Many of our customers have already experienced the benefits with skin elasticity, minimising the appearance of stretch marks and getting a radiant glow. Additionally, it promotes strong and healthy hair, already helping our customers to combat postpartum hair loss.

    For any specific concerns, we recommend consulting with your general practitioner, who can provide personalised advice tailored to your needs.
